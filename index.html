Introduction

Mary Miller Music Studio offers guitar, piano and voice lessons to music students of all skill levels and ages. Mary has a B.A. in Music Education K-12 from St. Olaf College and over 20 years of private teaching experience. Specializing in beginning students of all ages, Mary works with students to develop a deeper appreciation of music in general, along with improved technical skills. Please contact the studio if you have any questions or are interested in signing up for lessons!
